ABOUT MEADOW GOLD

Hawaii's Dairy Brand Since 1897

Meadow Gold has been a part of Hawaii's everyday life for over a century. From the iconic glass milk bottles of the early 1900s to the beloved Lani Moo character that generations of kamaʻāina grew up with, Meadow Gold is more than just dairy — it's a piece of Hawaii's heritage.

Today, Meadow Gold produces a wide range of products beloved across the islands: whole milk, chocolate milk, the classic POG juice, teas, ice cream, and more. Every sip connects Hawaii families to a tradition of quality and local pride.

  • Over 125 years serving Hawaii families
  • Home of the beloved Lani Moo mascot
  • Full lineup: milk, POG, teas, ice cream & more
  • Committed to local sourcing and community
🏈
ABOUT UH WARRIORS

The Pride of Mānoa

The University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa Warriors compete across 20 varsity sports, representing the islands on the national stage with aloha. From Rainbow Warriors football filling Clarence T.C. Ching Athletics Complex to the Rainbow Wahine volleyball dynasty, UH athletics is a source of statewide pride and community spirit.

UH athletes are more than competitors — they're role models for the next generation of Hawaii kids. This partnership gives fans a new way to connect with their favorite Warriors while celebrating the values of hard work, community, and local pride.

  • 20 varsity sports programs at UH Mānoa
  • Multiple national championships across sports
  • Deep ties to the Hawaii community and culture
  • A pipeline for local athletic talent to shine
THE CONCEPT

Why Collectible Cards?

Because community, repeat purchase, and athlete visibility all start with a story worth sharing.

🛒
Drive Repeat Purchases

Every gallon is a new chance to unlock a card. Collectors naturally buy more Meadow Gold to complete their set — turning a routine grocery run into an exciting hunt.

🌟
Spotlight UH Athletes

NIL partnerships and athlete visibility matter. This gives UH athletes a platform to be celebrated by a wider Hawaii audience beyond the stadium.

📊
Gather Consumer Data

The pre-reveal survey collects first-party consumer insights for Meadow Gold — household size, age group, product preferences — in a fun, non-intrusive way.

🤝
Build Community

The leaderboard, island challenge, and trading features create a shared experience that connects Meadow Gold fans with UH Warriors fans across the islands.

INNOVATE808 COMPETITION

Built for Hawaii's Premier Business Competition

This prototype was created for the Innovate808 business competition in Hawaii — a platform for students and entrepreneurs to pitch innovative business ideas that serve Hawaii's unique economy and community.

The Meadow Gold × UH Warriors Collect concept was designed to demonstrate how a beloved local brand could deepen consumer engagement through digital collectibles, NIL athlete partnerships, and gamified repeat-purchase mechanics — without any complex infrastructure or app store dependency.
