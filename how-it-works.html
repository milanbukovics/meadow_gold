FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Where do I find the 6-digit code?

The unique 6-digit code is printed on the inside surface of the cap on participating Meadow Gold gallon jugs. Unscrew the cap completely and look at the underside — you'll see the code stamped near the center.

Which Meadow Gold products are participating?

Currently, the promotion runs on Meadow Gold gallon jugs including whole milk, 2% milk, and chocolate milk sold at participating Hawaii retailers. Look for the green "Collect" promotional seal on the label to confirm the jug is participating.

Can I enter a code more than once?

Each 6-digit code is unique and can only be used one time. Once a code has been redeemed, it won't work again. But you can enter as many different codes as you collect — one per gallon!

Do I need to make a purchase?

No purchase is necessary to participate. Please see the official rules for details on how to obtain a code without purchase.

How do I redeem a prize card?

If you unlock a prize card, it will appear in your collection marked with a special prize badge. Follow the redemption instructions sent to your registered email after saving the card to your account. Prizes must be redeemed within 90 days of unlocking.

What happens if I get a duplicate card?

Duplicate cards still add to your collection count but won't overwrite an existing card. In a future update, we plan to add a card-trading feature so you can swap duplicates with other collectors in the community.

Is there a limit to how many codes I can enter?

There is no limit — enter as many codes as you collect. The more gallons you buy, the better your chances of completing the full 8-card set and finding a rare prize card!

Who are the UH athlete cards featuring?

The first series features UH Warriors athletes across Swimming, Volleyball, Football, Baseball, Women's Basketball, and Women's Soccer. Additional athletes will be added in future series as the program expands.
